Video retrieval

After pressing the "Search" button, a search will be started, where your task will be to find the template image shown in the bottom left corner.

Sending of photo, you think is the correct answer, is done by ctrl + click to that photo.

After double click on any photo will be displayed context of photo in dataset, but photo in context can't be sent as answer due to the currently tested method.

If you don't know what to do or if you don't want to search for the current image, you can always skip the search using the "Next" button.
